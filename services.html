Pricing

Cost for services vary but are dependent on one essential concept; the "Triple Constraint Model". In the TCM, scope, time, and cost are constraints that form the sides of the triangle, while quality of your product is the central theme.

The three constraints are interdependent: Modifying one constraint will affect one or both of the others. For example, if the scope of a project is increased, it is likely to take longer and/or cost more. Likewise, an earlier deadline is almost certain to either require more money or a less ambitious scope. Every project is different so communication and focus are key. It saves everyone time and money, all of which any growing organization needs.
